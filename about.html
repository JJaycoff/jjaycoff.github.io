About Baby Drones

Baby Drones are creatures that live in the minute cliff island of Wurl, in the middle of the ocean.

They've lived there for generations, ever since they evolved millenia ago. They know they can't go very far, though, after all they are limited to flying only three hours before they need to charge in the trees...

... and there aren't very many trees in the middle of the ocean
